About Me


A Front End Developer

I love to create beautiful, responsiveand functional websites, and I especially enjoy Codewars in my spare time!

DOWNLOAD CV or RESUME

My love for frontend development began in early 2020, when a number of my closest family members suffered serious health problems, and I found myself spending many months as a caregiver in remote hospital rooms. Although my career and source of comfort had always been the piano, I could not access any pianos from the hospital, and I was too distracted to focus on reading books. A friend of mine suggested that I build a website for our local teacher organization, and I thought that might be a great distraction while I was caring for my loved ones. So, as a total beginner, I first learned HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and by the time I had completed the website I knew that I had found another love. I enrolled in Team Treehouse to learn Front End Development, and later, while still teaching and caregiving full-time, I enrolled in TrueCoders to learn Software Engineering. At TrueCoders I learned C#, .NET, SQL, APIs, Test Driven Development, data structures and algorithms, and developed an unquenchable obsession with Codewars. I have loved every moment of learning and creating, and now that my caregiver responsibilities have ended, I am excited to pursue a scareer as a Frontend Developer.
